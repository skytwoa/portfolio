about

A chair must stand before looking cool.

I'm a product and graphic designer, born in southern Italy, based in the Netherlands. Curiosity and craftiness have been my drive since I was born. Later I decided to pursue a career as a designer, focusing on industrial products and visual compositions.

My workflow is straight and simple, and follows these four rules:


  • Research

    the users, live in their context, listen to the stories they are telling

  • Sketch

    concepts, always with a strict focus on feasability, never forgetting to experiment

  • Design

    , build, break, then all over again

  • Optimize

    functions, mechanisms and features that can be reduced and simplified

My projects look very different from each other, and this is what I'm after: unique functions and aesthetics as values for the brand, the market and the user.

Apart from design my interests range through music, science, cooking and videogames. In my spare time I also play drums, as a semi-pro.
